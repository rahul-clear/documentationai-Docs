## Welcome to Clear

Clear is your default documentation space. Organize and manage all project documentation in one centralized hub.

## What is Clear?

Clear provides a flexible platform for teams to create, collaborate on, and maintain documentation. Build structured guides, API references, and tutorials tailored to your projects.

Who should use Clear? Developers, product teams, and technical writers seeking an intuitive space to document workflows, APIs, and best practices.
