---
title: "How to add a VAT number to KSA E-Invoicing Account?"
description: "Step-by-step guide to add a new VAT number to your ClearTax E-Invoicing KSA account"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to your ClearTax account" icon="log-in">
    Sandbox - [https://app-sandbox.cleartax.com/middle-east/ksa/einvoicing](https://app-sandbox.cleartax.com/middle-east/ksa/einvoicing)

    Production account - [https://app.cleartax.com/middle-east/ksa/einvoicing](https://app.cleartax.com/middle-east/ksa/einvoicing/)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to the “Settings” page" icon="settings">
    After logging in you will land on the ‘Dashboard’ page of your account. Once you are on the account dashboard, click on the "Settings" option at the bottom of the left-side menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to “Business Settings” page" icon="briefcase">
    After reaching the ‘Settings’ page, click on “Business Settings” to open the list of VATs and branches in your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a new “Business”" icon="plus">
    On the right side of the ‘Business settings’ page there is a button to “ADD BUSINESS”. Click on that button, which opens up two options:

    1. Add New VAT — to add a new VAT
    2. Add Branches to existing VAT

    Click on ‘Add New VAT’ to start adding details of the new VAT in your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter details of your VAT and save" icon="edit">
    Enter the details as prompted. Organization name is a free text field for the name of your enterprise, while ‘Organization Unit Name’ will be your TIN if your VAT is a Group VAT, else it is also a free text field.

    After successfully adding the VAT number, Organization name, and Organization unit name (first ten digits of VAT), click "SAVE AND CONTINUE" to proceed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify the entered information" icon="check">
    After saving the business information, the new VAT number will be added to the ‘Business settings’ page. More branches can be added to this VAT if required, or you can proceed with the next set of actions as per your plan.
  </Step>
</Steps>
