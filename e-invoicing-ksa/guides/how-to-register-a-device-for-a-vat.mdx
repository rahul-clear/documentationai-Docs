---
title: "How to register a device for a VAT?"
description: "Step-by-step guide to onboard and register a device (Invoice Generation Solution Unit) for a VAT in ClearTax E-Invoicing KSA"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Generate OTP from the ERAD portal" icon="key">
    To register a device for a VAT, you first need to generate the OTP from the ERAD portal. Before generating the OTP, select the environment for which you want to register the device (Simulation / Production). See [How to generate OTP on ERAD Portal?](/e-invoicing-ksa/guides/how-to-generate-otp-on-erad-portal)

    Save the OTP for further processing.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log in to your ClearTax E-Invoicing account" icon="log-in">
    Sandbox Account Link - [https://app-sandbox.cleartax.com/middle-east/ksa/einvoicing](https://app-sandbox.cleartax.com/middle-east/ksa/einvoicing)

    Production Account Link - [https://app.cleartax.com/middle-east/ksa/einvoicing](https://app.cleartax.com/middle-east/ksa/einvoicing/)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Settings" icon="settings">
    On the bottom left corner, click on “Settings”.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Switch to Phase II" icon="toggle-right">
    On the sidebar menu, click on “Switch to phase II” and enable Phase 2 if it is not already enabled.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a new device" icon="plus">
    On the sidebar, click on Devices, then select “ONLINE DEVICES”, then click on “Add new devices”.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add device details" icon="edit">
    Add a unique Common Name/Device Name, Industry, EGS serial number, and Location. Select Invoice Type as per your requirement (Standard / Simplified / Both).

    Example EGS Number format: `1-TEST|2-CT|3-101`

    After filling in all the details, click on “Save and Continue”.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter the OTP" icon="lock">
    Enter the OTP saved from the ERAD Portal in step 1. The OTP must be entered within one hour of generation — after one hour it expires.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save and continue" icon="check">
    Click on “Save and Continue”. The registered devices list will show the status of each device (active or failed).
  </Step>
</Steps>
