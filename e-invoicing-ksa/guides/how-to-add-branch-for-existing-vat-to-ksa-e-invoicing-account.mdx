---
title: "How to add Branch for existing Vat to KSA E-Invoicing Account?"
description: "Step-by-step guide to add a branch under an existing VAT in your ClearTax E-Invoicing KSA account"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to your ClearTax account" icon="log-in">
    Sandbox - [https://app-sandbox.cleartax.com/middle-east/ksa/einvoicing](https://app-sandbox.cleartax.com/middle-east/ksa/einvoicing)

    Production account - [https://app.cleartax.com/middle-east/ksa/einvoicing](https://app.cleartax.com/middle-east/ksa/einvoicing/)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to “Settings”" icon="settings">
    On the bottom left corner, click on “Settings”.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Branch for existing VAT" icon="git-branch">
    On the sidebar menu, click on “Business Settings”, then click on the “Add Business” button, and then click on “Add Branches for existing Vat”. A modal window will open.

    Select the VAT for which you want to add a branch, add the Branch Name and Branch Code details, and then click Save.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Branch added successfully" icon="check">
    After clicking Save, you will get a notification that the branch was added successfully.
  </Step>
</Steps>
