## E-invoicing Onboarding

**Scope of Services for E-invoicing Onboarding**

ClearTax will conduct 2 hours of Onboarding and training session. The session will include:

* Feature walkthrough of the ClearTax web platform.
* Training on using the excel template for uploading data on ClearTax.
* Device registration and account setup on the ClearTax platform.
