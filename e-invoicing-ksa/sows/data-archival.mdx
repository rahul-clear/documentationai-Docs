## Data Archival and Storage

1. Taxpayers can opt for storage of invoices on ClearTax cloud as per their requirements. For example: if the taxpayer has opted for an archival/storage period of 1 year, then the data will be stored by the ClearTax platform for 1 year, post which the data will be purged.
2. All invoice data will be available for download and use via reports and PDFs during the archival/storage period opted for by the taxpayer.
3. All invoice data beyond the opted archival/storage period will be purged from ClearTax databases and will be unavailable for any use. Taxpayers are required to download the data before the completion of the storage period and store it on their systems.
4. The data available on ClearTax systems will be stored as per the data archival and storage policies of ClearTax, which are as below:

   1. **Active use** — Documents where the difference between the document date and current date is less than 90 days will be available for active use. Taxpayers can see it in the 'Documents' view and perform all actions on it.
   2. **Infrequent use** — Documents where the difference between the document date and current date is more than 90 days but less than 365 days will be available for infrequent use. Data can be accessed from the 'Archives' page via reports and PDF download. Typically the download of the reports would take 45 mins to 120 mins depending on the size of data.
   3. **Archived** — Documents where the difference between the document date and current date is more than 365 days will be archived, as the usage would be very low. Data can be accessed from the 'Archives' page via reports and PDF download. Typically the download of the reports would take 8 hours to 16 hours depending on the size of data.
