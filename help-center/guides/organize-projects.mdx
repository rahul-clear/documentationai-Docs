## Overview

Create folders and pages to organize your project docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a folder">
    Click **New Folder** in your project's sidebar. Name it by topic, team, or feature.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a page">
    Inside a folder, click **New Page**. Write and format your documentation content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rearrange items">
    Drag folders and pages to adjust the order for easy navigation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
