---
title: Cannot log in to Clear
description: Quick fixes for Clear login issues.
---

## Symptoms

- Login form does not accept credentials
- Error: "Invalid username or password"

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check credentials">
    Make sure your email and password are correct (case-sensitive).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset password">
    Click **Forgot Password** on the login screen to get a reset link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browser issues">
    Try clearing cookies or switching browsers.
  </Step>
</Steps>
